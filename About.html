About SavorStop

The SavorStop group is the leading global online food delivery marketplace. It operates in more than 20 countries across 5 continents.

SavorStop allows users to conveniently discover food around their neighborhood and directly order their favorite meals online or via mobile.

In the Philippines, SavorStop was launched in August 2024 and has since grown to over 1,000 partner restaurants nationwide.

We are a small team of problem solvers, designers, thinkers, and tinkers, working around the clock to make SavorStop the most powerful online tool for food delivery. We believe that ordering food should be easy, fast, and definitely fun!

Tired of the same take-out for lunch? Too lazy to go out for dinner? Simply download the SavorStop app or go to www.SavorStop.ph, and see which restaurants deliver to you. Food delivery has never been easier!
